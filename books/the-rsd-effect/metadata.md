## Short Blurb
Rejection Sensitive Dysphoria (RSD) is one of the most painful and misunderstood experiences of the ADHD/neurodivergent brain. This book explains the neuroscience behind why rejection hurts so much and provides practical strategies for riding the emotional wave, quieting the pain, and reclaiming your confidence. No "grow a thicker skin" advice—just real tools for real sensitivity.
