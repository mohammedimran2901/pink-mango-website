## Short Blurb
Women with ADHD are 3-6x more likely to develop eating disorders. This practical toolkit bridges the gap between ADHD and disordered eating—covering binge eating, restriction, ARFID, medication effects, dopamine seeking, executive dysfunction, interoception, and body image. No diet culture. No willpower lectures. ADHD-friendly strategies that actually work.
