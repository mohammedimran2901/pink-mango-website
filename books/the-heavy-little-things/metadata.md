## Short Blurb
Complex trauma is not a single event - it's a sustained environment. This practical toolkit for women covers CPTSD from the ground up: understanding your nervous system, working with your inner self, regulating emotional flashbacks, healing relationships, setting boundaries, and building a life that feels safe. Gentle, trauma-informed, and actionable.
