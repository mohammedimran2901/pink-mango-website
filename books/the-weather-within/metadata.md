## Book Description (Short)

> Your moods are not your enemy. They are weather — and weather can be forecast, prepared for, and ridden out.
>
> *The Weather Within* reframes bipolar management around the metaphor of weather patterns: climate, forecast, and current conditions. Written for women, it covers the full toolkit — mood tracking, medication partnership, sleep hygiene, hypomania containment, depression navigation, relationships, career, hormones, and crisis planning. No theory. Just tools.
