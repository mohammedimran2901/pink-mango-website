## Short Blurb
PMDD is not "bad PMS." It's a severe neuroendocrine condition affecting 3-8% of women. This practical toolkit covers everything from symptom tracking and luteal phase survival strategies to treatment options, relationships, work, and self-compassion. Evidence-based, validating, and actionable.
